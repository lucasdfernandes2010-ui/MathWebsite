Purpose of this Website

  • You can solve past papers from math olympiads like AMC 8, AMC 10, AMC 12, AIME, USAMO, IOQM, RMO, INMO, and IMO
  • You can read books and learning resources provided by me
  • You can check the syllabi of major math olympiads
  • You can check syllabi and past papers for the ISI and CMI undergraduate math entrance exams
  • To recommend you random questions
  • To tell you random quotes
  • Making you better at math is my top priority, that's why I don't even have Ads
